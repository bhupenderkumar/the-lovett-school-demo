Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4, focusing on social-emotional learning, literacy, and math skills. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that encourages exploration and creativity.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program serves students in grades K-5, offering a well-rounded education that includes language arts, math, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes allow for personalized attention and support.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs serve students in grades 6-12, offering a range of academic and extracurricular opportunities. Our students are challenged to think critically, solve problems, and develop their passions.

🤖

STEM

Our STEM program offers a comprehensive education in science, technology, engineering, and math, preparing students for careers in these fields. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty provide a supportive and engaging learning environment.

🎨

Arts

Our arts program offers a range of courses and activities in music, drama, dance, and visual arts. Our students have the opportunity to express themselves creatively and develop their talents in a supportive and encouraging environment.

🏈

Athletics

Our athletic program offers a range of sports and activities for students in grades 6-12, promoting teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness. Our experienced coaches and trainers provide guidance and support to help our students achieve their goals.
